About Me

Resilient and highly organized software engineer with a passion for cutting-edge technology and a commitment to continuous learning. Currently pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and Game Development with a minor in Web Development at the University of Southern California, and am in my third year of studies.


My focus on Software Engineering is driven by a strong analytical mindset and a desire to delve deep into the complexities of developing robust and efficient solutions. My various experiences have equipped me with hands-on proficiency in areas such as algorithm design, system architecture, and debugging techniques. From coding to testing, I thrive on mastering the intricacies of the complete software development lifecycle.


In the realm of entrepreneurship, my free time becomes a playground for coding ventures and strategic problem-solving. I immerse myself in the dynamic landscape of software engineering challenges, continually honing my skills through hands-on projects that push the boundaries of innovation. From diving into algorithmic complexities to exploring the intersections of AI and design, I'm passionate about leveraging technology to create meaningful solutions.


Beyond the screen, I'm not just your typical coding maestro. You'll find me maintaining a healthy lifestyle, whether it's hitting the gym or exploring natural parks to recharge my mind. Off-duty, I love traveling to new cities with my boyfriend, wrapping up each adventure with a few episodes of South Park.


As a dedicated and determined student, I am actively seeking opportunities that will allow me to further advance my skills as a Software Engineer, all while enjoying every step of the way.
